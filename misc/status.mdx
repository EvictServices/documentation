Evict maintains a public status page where you can check the real-time operational status of its services. 

## Accessing the status page

Visit [evict.bot/status](https://evict.bot/status) to view the current status of Evict services.
The status page displays the operational status of all clusters. 


## Reporting issues

If you're experiencing issues that aren't reflected on the status page, contact Evict support through the [Evict Discord server](https://discord.gg/evict).
